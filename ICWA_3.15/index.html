Indian Child Welfare Act

Walking With ICWA

Learn the Indian Child Welfare Act through conversation, real scenarios, and hands-on exploration. This is for everyone: attorneys, social workers, tribal members, families, and anyone wanting to understand ICWA.

A note before you begin ICWA is a federal law, not a racial classification. It recognizes the political relationship between tribes and the federal government, and the right of tribes to protect their children and communities.
